What is the Cloud?

What is "the cloud" really? Understand cloud computing through the simple analogy of renting an apartment instead of buying a house.

The cloud is just someone else's computer. Instead of buying and maintaining your own servers, you rent computing power from companies like Amazon (AWS), Microsoft (Azure), or Google (GCP).

The Apartment Analogy

Buying a House (On-Premise Servers):

  • You own it completely
  • High upfront costs
  • You handle all maintenance and repairs
  • Fixed space - can't easily expand

Renting an Apartment (Cloud Computing):

  • Pay only for what you use
  • Low upfront costs
  • Landlord handles maintenance
  • Easy to upgrade or downgrade
